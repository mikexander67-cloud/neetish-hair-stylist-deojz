Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

A few of the questions clients ask most often — about booking, services, and what to expect from your appointment.

Booking is online and takes about a minute. Use the Book Appointment button anywhere on this site — it opens the live calendar where you can pick a date, time and service that suits you. If you'd prefer to chat first, send a quick message on WhatsApp and we'll guide you from there.
Signature cuts and styling, colour and highlights (balayage, full colour, root touch-ups, toning), bridal hair with pre-wedding trials, hair treatments (gloss, repair, bond-building, scalp), blowouts and event styling, plus consultations for extensions. If you're not sure which service fits, send a message and we'll point you in the right direction.
Not necessarily. Every appointment begins with a short consultation in the chair, so we can talk through what you're after and shape the service around you. For major colour changes, bridal styling, or extensions, a separate consultation beforehand is recommended — it gives us proper time to plan.
It depends on the service. A cut and style is usually around 60–90 minutes, colour services can run two to three hours, and bridal styling is booked as a longer dedicated session. We never double-book, so the time you reserve is genuinely yours.
Pricing is shared during your consultation, because hair is genuinely personal — length, density, colour history and what you're trying to achieve all influence the final price. Send a WhatsApp message with a quick description of what you're looking for and we'll give you a clear, honest estimate before you commit.
Come with your hair clean but not freshly washed the same morning — day-old hair styles best. For colour services, please don't use box dye or heavy build-up products in the week before. If you have inspiration photos, bring them along; they're genuinely useful for shaping the conversation.
You'll get tailored aftercare advice at the end of every appointment, including which products will suit your hair and routine. As a starting point: wash less often than you think, use a heat protectant before styling, and book a treatment between bigger services to keep condition strong.
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice to reschedule or cancel, so the slot can be offered to someone else. Cancellations inside 24 hours, or no-shows, may require a small deposit to secure your next booking. For bridal appointments, longer notice is appreciated due to the time set aside.
Yes — bridal hair is a quiet specialty. Every bridal booking includes a pre-wedding trial so we can refine the look, test how it holds, and make sure it suits your dress and venue. For other events — parties, photoshoots, milestone moments — book a blowout and event styling appointment.
The studio is based in Mauritius and is appointment-only. The exact address and parking notes are shared by WhatsApp once your booking is confirmed, so the space stays private for the client in the chair.
