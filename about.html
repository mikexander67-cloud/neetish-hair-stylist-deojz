About

Crafted to make you feel beautiful.

A private hairstyling studio built around one belief — that hair is personal, and so is the way it should be cared for.

Neetish Hair Stylist began with a quiet idea — that every client deserves a stylist who listens first, and works with care second.

What started as a passion for hair has grown into a small, private studio in Mauritius — appointment-only, intentionally unhurried, and entirely focused on the person in the chair. No conveyor-belt salon energy, no rushing between three clients at once. Just considered work.

Every appointment begins with a real conversation. About your hair, your routine, what's worked and what hasn't, and the way you actually wear your hair on a normal Tuesday. From there, the cut, colour or treatment is shaped to suit you — not a trend, not a template.

The result is hair that holds up beautifully between visits. The kind of finish that earns quiet compliments, lasts longer than a single occasion, and leaves you feeling unmistakably yourself.
